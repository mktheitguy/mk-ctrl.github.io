About

More About Me.

I'm Mehmood Khalid, a freelance web developer based in Montreal, specializing in full-stack web development, responsive design, and custom web applications. With 7+ years of experience, I transform ideas into fast, scalable, and user-friendly digital solutions.

My journey in tech began when discovered a passion for solving problems through code. Since then, I’ve worked with startups and established companies in Montreal, delivering projects that blend clean design with robust functionality. Currently based in Montreal, I’m available for freelance projects and consultations. Let’s discuss how I can help bring your vision to life whether it’s a website revamp, a custom web app, or ongoing technical support.
